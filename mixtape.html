Secret Sign - Jay Chou

Oct 12 2014

Jay Chou is one of the most famous artists among Asia. His song covers all my high school and college life. I am not sure when this song released, but it reminds me the senior year of my high school. Every high school student in Taiwan shares the same memory. We go to cramschool around Taipei Railroad Station three times or four times a week to prepare the coming college entrance exam. We hung out with friends around that area. We went to record stores, stationary stores, and we took those 'photo stickers' a lot. It was one of my best times in life. I was so fearless, energetic, and hopeful. Life is full of possibilities.
